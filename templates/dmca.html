DMCA Safe Harbor

{{ SITE_NAME }} complies with the DMCA safe-harbor provisions. To qualify:

  • No video content is stored by {{ SITE_NAME }}; we only relay streams from external platforms.
  • We respond promptly to valid DMCA takedown notices.
  • We have no control over third-party content.
  • Rights holders have clear contact options to report copyright violations.

Submitting a DMCA Takedown Notice

To report an alleged copyright infringement, contact us using the support options available at the bottom of this page. Please include:

  • Full legal name of the copyright owner or authorized representative
  • Email address
  • City and country
  • URLs of the infringing content
  • Description of the copyrighted work that is allegedly infringed
  • Description of the infringement
  • Statement confirming the information is accurate and that you are authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner

Misrepresenting copyright claims can have legal consequences under Section 512(f) of the DMCA or similar laws. {{ SITE_NAME }} will promptly review valid reports and remove content when appropriate.
